How It Works

Everything Runs on Autopilot

Six critical billing operations handled in real-time without writing backend code.

🔗

Webhook Processing

Receive and validate Razorpay webhook events in real-time. Every event is logged, deduplicated, and routed instantly.

🚀

Auto Activation

New subscriptions auto-create user accounts, link plans, set access tiers, and send welcome emails — zero manual work.

💳

Payment Tracking

Every successful charge is recorded with amounts, currencies, and receipt URLs. Failed payments trigger instant alerts.

📧

Smart Emails

Automated receipts, failure warnings, cancellation confirmations, and trial-ending reminders — all sent at the right moment.

🔄

Retry & Recovery

Failed internal processing is automatically retried every 10 minutes. No events are ever lost or silently dropped.

📊

Daily Reconciliation

A daily sync compares your database with Razorpay to catch and fix any drifted states. Mismatches are corrected and logged.
